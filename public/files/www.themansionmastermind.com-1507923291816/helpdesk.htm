    Most Asked Questions
    What do I do when I sale an item on Amazon

    The training is in the E-com Blue Print Program . You process the order at the supplier website and they will send it to your customers.

    I sold one item where the price changed on me. If I can't find it anywhere else for less, would you cancel the order or take a small loss on the order to make the customer happy?

    First try to find the item with a different supplier. It's best to fulfill the order even at a lost but that lost will be dependent on you. For example, $10 lost on a product, ok I will process the order. $100 lost on a product, I cancel the order.

    How can I tell if the product is incorrect (not the same)?

    Brand, Pictures, Size, Color, Model Number. Basically if you're not sure if the item is the same or not, it's not the same and it's incorrect that item.
