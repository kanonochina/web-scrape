The Challenge To Change Lives

Coach Giani’s life has been an open book for everyone who got to know for the past years. His story has been an inspiration to many because he has gone through the same trials that any of us are facing right now. Despite the achievements that he has already achieved right now, he did not forget to return the help that was once extended to him by his mentors.

The Millionaire Mastermind has put up several contests that entitled people to join in even if they had nothing to start with. They have watched and followed Coach Giani on his YouTube and other social media accounts that made them aware of the contests that are going on. There was a time when he put up a contest that will entitle one to become his student and will be mentored by him.

Just recently, Coach Giani put up a 30-Day Challenge that encourages everyone to join to learn and get value out of the challenge that they do every day. He had his team built a site for this in order for them to have an account where they will do the challenge. The mechanics are just simple and the contestants can have as much as 80 points per day.

Although the winner gets a reward out of joining the challenge, they will not miss the fact that they will learn from it. Joining the 30-Day Challenge gives them a glimpse of how the marketing world works and how to face the challenges when confronted. Running a business by learning new strategies and skills is very challenging especially that doing business online is far more different than the traditional ones. Doing online business needs a mentor for you to know the needed set of skills.

Coach Giani has always emphasized the importance of mentoring in order to know the basics. Because of that, he makes himself available for mentoring his students and the videos he does every day is just the shallow part of it. Joining the challenge is a good start for building your business online and the rest follows it.
