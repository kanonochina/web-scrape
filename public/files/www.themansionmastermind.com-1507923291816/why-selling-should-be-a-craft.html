Why Selling Should Be A Craft?

They say that selling is one of the toughest jobs in the world. This is a job that most people won’t get into because they believe that job is not easy. Well, the truth is that no job is easy at all, especially if you do not have the skills and the mastery. And the truth about marketing or sales is that people are hindered by the thought of being rejected.

With the fact that rejection is a part of life, there is nothing to be afraid of it. Sales is an integral part of every business. A company that is not making sales will never survive. If you take a look at any aspect, selling is involved. Like when applying for work, you sell yourself in order to get the job. Because of selling, people have jobs and companies have profits.

Coach Giani believes that selling is a thing that one shouldn’t be afraid of. In order to conquer one’s fear of rejection, he made this as his challenge on the 19th day. He encourages everyone who joins the challenge to make a sale without thinking of rejection that they might get. Whether they make a sale or not, it doesn’t matter. The thing that matters is that everyone accepts the challenge and start looking at selling in a different perspective.

We may all get a “NO” when trying to make a sale but that doesn’t define the skills that you have for selling. Closing a sale may be that tough but while you are learning things over time, the efforts in closing a sale gets lesser. All skills can be learned and selling is not an exemption to it. If you know how to accept defeat, there is no way you can’t accept rejection. It is by getting rejections that you learn to strategize in order to get a “YES” from your clients next time.

Building a business isn’t easy if you do not know how to sell your stuff. That is the reason why Coach Giani wants you to take the challenge of closing a sale before building an online business so that you will have revenues. Doing the things you haven’t done before is something you should be afraid of. If you just take things positively, you will see the adventure it brings until such time that you know how it’s done.
